Meenakshi Temple

The Meenakshi Temple in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar,showcasing stunning Dravidian architecture. Its majestic gopurams with intricate, colorful sculptures make it a world-famous spiritual and cultural landmark.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, or Harmandir Sahib, in Amritsar, Punjab, is the holiest shrine of Sikhism, symbolizing equality and humility. Its golden-plated structure, surrounded by the serene Amrit Sarovar, attracts millions of devotees and visitors worldwide.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located on the banks of the Ganga, it is a revered pilgrimage site symbolizing spiritual liberation and devotion.

Sri Venkateswara Temple

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It is a stunning white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Brihadeeswara Temple

The Brihadeeswara Temple in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, is a UNESCO World Heritage site dedicated to Lord Shiva. Built by Raja Raja Chola I, it is renowned for its massive vimana (tower) and grand Dravidian architecture.

Jagannath temple

The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha, is a major Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu. It is famous for the annual Rath Yatra, where the deities are taken out on grand chariots.
