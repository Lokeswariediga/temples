Sri Venkateswara Temple

The Sri Venkateswara Temple, located in Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh, is one of the most visited and richest Hindu temples in the world. It is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, believed to have appeared here to save mankind. Tirumala is a major pilgrimage destination, attracting millions of devotees annually. The famous Laddu Prasadam offered here is cherished by visitors worldwide. The temple follows elaborate daily rituals and festivals, including the grand Brahmotsavam. It is managed by the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) trust. The serene hilltop location adds to its divine atmosphere and scenic beauty. Sri Venkateswara Temple stands as a symbol of unwavering devotion, tradition, and cultural pride.
